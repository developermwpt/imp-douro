2. What Data We Collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect:

3. Why We Collect Your Data

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Respond to your service inquiry promptly

  • Improve our services and customer experience

We do not use your data for profiling, automated decision-making, or targeted advertising.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent — by submitting the contact form you consent to us processing your data to respond to your inquiry.
  • Legitimate Interest — we have a legitimate business interest in managing customer inquiries and maintaining operational records.
  • Legal Obligation — we may retain certain records as required by UAE commercial and tax regulations.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With email service providers used solely to deliver our response to you.
  • When legally required by UAE authorities or courts.
  • With your explicit consent for any other purpose.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain contact form data for up to 3 years for business record-keeping and follow-up purposes. After this period, data is securely deleted. Your language preference is stored only in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Under the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection (PDPL), you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Ask us to correct any inaccurate information.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.

Right to Restrict Processing

Ask us to limit how we use your data.

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Object to the processing of your data, including for marketing.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time, without penalty.

How to Exercise Your Rights

Email us at douroimp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission and restricted access to customer records. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a child has submitted data, we will delete it promptly.
